## Scoring System Overview

**Thresholds:**
- 🔥 **HOT (75-100)**: Highly Qualified | Contact within 1 hour
- 🟡 **WARM (55-74)**: Qualified | Contact within 4 hours  
- 🔵 **COOL (35-54)**: Needs Nurturing | Follow up in 24 hours
- ❄️ **COLD (0-34)**: Low Intent | Nurture sequence, 48hr follow-up

**Weighting:**
- Timeline: **25%** (strongest urgency indicator)
- Travel: **20%** (must be willing to cross border)
- Journey: **15%** (experience level + pain points)
- Location: **10%** (geographic proximity to Tijuana)
- Clinic: **10%** (current status + switching intent)
- Path: **10%** (multi-cycle interest = higher LTV)
- Age: **5%** (medical factor but less controllable)
- Concern: **5%** (context for messaging, not qualification)

## 📊 KEY FINDINGS

### ✅ What Creates HOT Leads (75+):
1. **Timeline urgency** (ASAP or 1-3 months) — weighted 25%
2. **Strong travel commitment** (Yes, definitely) — weighted 20%
3. **Failed cycles or clinic shopping** (high pain = high intent) — weighted 15%
4. **Geographic proximity** (SoCal, Arizona, N. Mexico) — weighted 10%
5. **Multi-cycle interest** (higher LTV) — weighted 10%

### 🟡 What Creates WARM Leads (55-74):
1. **Mid-range timeline** (1-6 months)
2. **Conditional travel** (Yes, if feels right)
3. **Active research phase** (looking for advanced options, comparing clinics)
4. **Cost concern** (perfect for Fertilite's value prop)

### 🔵 What Creates COOL Leads (35-54):
1. **Longer timeline** (3-6 months or just researching)
2. **Travel hesitancy** (Maybe, need info)
3. **Low urgency concerns** (not sure yet, logistics)
4. **Distance from geography** (Other US, International)

### ❄️ What Creates COLD Leads (0-34):
1. **No timeline** (just researching)
2. **Won't travel** (No)
3. **Far geography** + low urgency combination
4. **No clear path interest**

## 💡 RECOMMENDATIONS

### Scoring is Working Well ✅
- Failed cycle patients correctly flagged as HOT
- Travel willingness appropriately weighted (can't serve won't-travel leads)
- Timeline creates proper urgency hierarchy
- Multi-cycle interest boosts score (higher LTV)

### Consider Adjusting:
1. **Age weighting (currently 5%)**: Consider increasing to 7-8% since it impacts medical urgency
2. **Journey "Just starting" (currently 60)**: May be too low—some first-timers are highly qualified
3. **Location "Other US" (currently 60)**: Could segment further (TX/NM higher than NY/FL)

### Database Query Recommendations:
```sql
-- Get hot leads for immediate follow-up
SELECT * FROM leads 
WHERE lead_score >= 75 
AND status = 'new'
ORDER BY created_at DESC;

-- Get warm leads for same-day outreach
SELECT * FROM leads 
WHERE lead_score BETWEEN 55 AND 74 
AND status = 'new'
ORDER BY lead_score DESC, created_at DESC;

-- Get multi-cycle interested leads (higher LTV)
SELECT * FROM leads 
WHERE path_interest = 'multi_cycle'
AND lead_score >= 55
ORDER BY lead_score DESC;

-- Failed cycle patients (high intent, high pain)
SELECT * FROM leads 
WHERE ivf_stage LIKE '%failed%'
ORDER BY lead_score DESC;
```
